About me

Ocean Ahoy!

I grew up by the sea in Lunenburg, Nova Scotia, Canada for a good part of my young life, which is where I acquired my love of music. I started playing guitar, and eventually went on to complete a Bachelor of Music with a concentration in Music Education in 2011 at Acadia University.

While music fulfills me a great deal and I still continue to play, I always had a burning desire to learn more about computers and the way they work, so in 2010 I picked up my first book on Java Programming. Fast forward four years and I'm currently in my final year of my Bachelor of Science with an Honours Specialization in Computer Science at the University of Western Ontario.

The Future

I plan to finish my Honours Specialization in Computer Science in April 2014 and hope to begin my career as a software developer thereafter. My main areas of interest are working on web based applications and Android & iOS development. My interests are always expanding as I learn new skills and technologies, and I'm excited to see where my career will take me!
